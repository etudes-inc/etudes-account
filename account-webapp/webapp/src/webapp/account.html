Your Linked in account has a "public profile url". To find or edit this:
  • Login to LinkedIn
  • Edit your Profile
  • Note your public profile URL displayed under your profile picture

When you set your custom profile URL, you will see a dialog like this:

Learn about customizing LinkedIn's public profile URLs.

Enter the unique portion of the public profile URL here (the part in the box). You may also enter the full public profile URL, and Etudes will extract the unique portion for you.

  • Note your current username

When you then click on "Edit Username", you can view and change your username:

Learn more about Facebook's usernames.
